OUR STORY

From humble beginnings to Jamshedpur's most trusted luxury catering service.

It all began in 2000 when Sunil, a passionate foodie and visionary, started his journey to bring authentic Indian flavors to his community in Jamshedpur. With an unwavering commitment to quality and taste, Sunil Hi Tea Caterer grew from a small venture into a trusted name in the catering industry.

Our mission has always been simple: to offer delicious, high-quality, and authentic Indian cuisine, perfect for every occasion. Whether it's a wedding, a corporate event, or a private gathering, we pride ourselves on our attention to detail, presentation, and customer satisfaction.

Over the years, we've refined our catering services, added new dishes to our menu, and expanded our offerings to include everything from traditional feasts to contemporary twists on classic dishes.
